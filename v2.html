Roasted Vegetables

Perfect for weeknight dinners. Toss your favorite vegetables in olive oil and roasted until caramelized.

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups assorted vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Toss vegetables with olive oil and seasonings
  3. Spread on baking sheet
  4. Roast for 25 minutes until golden

Chicken Marinade

A versatile marinade that works with any cooking method. Perfect for grilling, baking, or pan-searing.

Marinating Time: 2-4 hours | Makes: 1 cup marinade

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons dried herbs (thyme, oregano)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix well and taste for balance
  3. Pour over chicken and refrigerate
  4. Marinate for at least 2 hours

Pasta Sauce

A classic tomato-based sauce that's ready in 30 minutes. Great for any pasta shape.

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Makes: 3 cups

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pinch of sugar

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot
  2. Sauté garlic until fragrant
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and seasonings
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally

Grilled Fish

A light and healthy option that's perfect for summer grilling. Works best with firm white fish.

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 fish fillets (salmon, halibut, or cod)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt, pepper, and fresh herbs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  2. Brush fish with olive oil and lemon juice
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs
  4. Grill for 6 minutes per side

Stir Fry

Quick, healthy, and customizable. Perfect for using up vegetables in your fridge.

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 3

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups mixed vegetables (snap peas, carrots, broccoli)
  • 1 pound protein (chicken, shrimp, or tofu)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced

Instructions:

  1. Heat wok or large skillet over high heat
  2. Cook protein first, set aside
  3. Stir fry vegetables for 5 minutes
  4. Return protein, add soy sauce and sesame oil
  5. Toss everything together and serve

Salad Dressing

A versatile vinaigrette that complements any salad. Make it fresh whenever you need it.

Prep Time: 3 minutes | Makes: 1/2 cup

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a jar
  2. Shake vigorously until emulsified
  3. Taste and adjust seasonings
  4. Use immediately or store in refrigerator

Soup Base

A foundational recipe that you can customize with your favorite vegetables and proteins.

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes: 6 cups

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups broth (vegetable or chicken)
  • 2 cups diced vegetables
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot
  2. Sauté onion and garlic until softened
  3. Add broth and vegetables
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes until vegetables are tender

Baked Goods

A basic formula for cookies, cakes, and breads. Perfect for experimenting with flavors.

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Cream butter and sugar together
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract
  4. Mix in flour, baking powder, and salt
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until golden

Smoothies

Refreshing drinks that double as a healthy breakfast or post-workout snack.

Prep Time: 2 minutes | Serves: 1-2

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen fruit (berries, mango, or pineapple)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon nut butter (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add frozen fruit to blender
  2. Add yogurt, milk, and honey
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately

Food Storage

Proper storage techniques to keep your food fresh and reduce waste.

Learn: Best practices for different food types

Storage Guidelines:

  • Refrigerator: 35-40°F for most items
  • Freezer: 0°F or below for long-term storage
  • Pantry: Cool, dry, and dark for shelf-stable items
  • Vegetables: Separate ethylene producers from ethylene sensitive items

Common Timeframes:

  1. Cooked meals: 3-4 days in refrigerator
  2. Raw vegetables: 5-7 days in crisper drawer
  3. Frozen items: 2-3 months for best quality
  4. Pantry items: Check expiration dates regularly
