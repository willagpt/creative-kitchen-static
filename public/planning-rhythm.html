03 · The planning cascade

What gets decided at which meeting

You're not inventing content each week. Decisions cascade from monthly (big) to weekly (tiny). By Friday morning, the only thing you're doing is saying the words.

whenwhat gets decidedoutput
Monthly
1st Thu · 2 hrs		 Kill fatigued ads. Pick the month's hero offer. Lock 4 Friday C2 topics + 2 Wednesday shot lists. "Creative plan · [Month] 2026" doc: kill list, offer, 4 script topics, 2 Wed meal menus, C1 testimonial angles, C4 offer variants.
Bi-weekly
Sun before shoot · 30 min		 Print shot sheets for Wednesday. Prep meals. Dress sets. Charge kit. Ready-to-shoot kitchen + desk on Wed morning. No Wed morning scrambling.
Weekly
Thu afternoon · 15 min		 Write Friday's C2 script from this week's Meta data + customer reviews. Kaja one-line approval. A3 script card on the desk for Friday morning. Nothing to decide when you sit down.
Shoot day
90 min or 6 hrs		 Nothing. Execute. The decisions are already made. Raw clips uploaded to Drive. The automation handles the rest.
The golden ruleIf you're making a creative decision on shoot day, something went wrong upstream. The shoot is execution, not design. Design happens at the Thursday desk.
04 · What the machine does

The automated half (Creative Kitchen Video)

You own a tool built exactly for this. Creative Kitchen Video already does the editing, voiceover, rendering, and Meta push. You don't need Remotion or a new stack. You just need to shoot the raw clips and drop them in a Drive folder.

The weekly C2 pipeline, end to end

  1. Fri 09:30 to 11:00 · James shoots 3 to 5 script variants. 2 cameras, 2 takes each. ~15 clips.
  2. Fri 11:00 · Kaja drops the folder into the CK-Video Google Drive share.
  3. Fri 11:15 onward · CK-Video auto-ingests, tags clips by type (HOOK / BODY / CTA), inserts into the clips table.
  4. Fri afternoon · For each script, the recipe builder auto-assembles hook → body → CTA → music bed and queues the render.
  5. Sat overnight · FFmpeg WASM renders every variant. Output lands in rendered_videos status complete.
  6. Sun 10:00 · 15 min coffee · James thumbs through the thumbnails. Approve the good ones.
  7. Mon 06:00 · push-ads fires approved videos into Meta. Campaign + adset + ad created. Activated.
  8. Tue onwards · sync-meta-insights pulls performance data. auto-review-ads flags fatigue. Feeds next Thursday's script decisions.
Your time per week for C290 min shoot + 5 min script + 15 min Sunday approval = under 2 hours for 3 to 5 fresh creatives entering Meta rotation every week. The bi-weekly Wednesday shoot is the same pipeline, just wider: 6 hrs + 30 min approval = 6.5 hrs for 15 to 20 creatives.
Small build needed (half day of engineering)Three things CK-Video doesn't do yet, all small: (1) auto-tag clips by type on upload via Claude vision, (2) 5 locked recipe templates (one per concept) so variants generate with one click, (3) a "render 3 variants from this brief" button in the Recipes view. Ticket this separately if you want the Sunday-morning approval flow to actually work.
