Concept 01 · Priority 1 · Dialogue-led
Kaja chest-framed testimonial
Kaja talks directly to camera at chest height, phone handheld, natural daylight. The emotional driver: "I stopped thinking about food for the first time in years." Grounded, quiet, no music. Earns its cut.
Duration: 20 to 30s final
Format: 9:16 vertical
Location: Home kitchen
Talent: Kaja
Lead cam: iPhone 17 Pro Max (handheld by James)
Reference: Simmer ad 1 (quiet handheld monologue, chest-framed) + Megan Petite Coach ad 3 (relief as the emotional payoff). Both scored for intimacy over polish. This one should feel found, not produced.

Concept 02 · Priority 1 · Dialogue-led

Format: 9:16 primary, 1:1 crop for cover
Location: Office desk
Talent: James
Lead cam: iPhone 15 Pro Max on tripod
