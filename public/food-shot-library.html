01 · How to use this

Three ways to get something useful in under a minute

If you're briefing a shoot, use the primary food tab. If you're looking for reference moods, filter by food state. If you're hunting a specific product, use the search box.

01

Briefing a shoot

Open Primary food. Filter by Meal category (e.g. savoury-main) and Food state (plated). You'll see ~150 hero food frames across all three brands. Click any card to copy the description as a reference line.

02

Looking for a mood

Skip the tabs. Pick one Food state chip (cooking, eating, packaging) and one Layout chip (full frame, split-2, split-3). You'll get a tight grid of visually similar references you can show a DOP in 10 seconds.

03

Spotting gaps

Use the brand chips to isolate a single competitor, then scan what meal categories they dominate. Frive owns breakfast bowls. Simmer owns cooking hands. Calo.UK barely shows raw prep. Go fill the gaps they left.
